About the Founder

Katie is an experienced psychotherapist in the field of court-ordered treatment for sexual offenders dedicated to preventing sexual abuse. Katie is a Licensed Social Worker with a Master's in Social Work and a Master's in Human Sexuality Education from Widener University.

She obtained two Bachelor's Degree in Psychology and Women/Gender Studies from the University of Dayton.
